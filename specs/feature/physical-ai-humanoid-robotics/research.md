- Sim-to-real transfer methods

### e) Vision-Language-Action (VLA)
- LLM integration for robotics
- Voice-to-action translation (e.g., OpenAI Whisper)
- Cognitive planning: translating commands into ROS 2 sequences
- Multi-modal AI for perception and action
