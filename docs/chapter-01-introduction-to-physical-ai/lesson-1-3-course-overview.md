# Lesson 1.3: Course Overview & Learning Outcomes

This course introduces students to Physical AI systems and covers:
- ROS 2 architecture and programming
- Simulation in Gazebo and Unity
- NVIDIA Isaac AI integration
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) pipelines
- Capstone project building an autonomous humanoid robot

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Understand concepts of Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence
- Program ROS 2 nodes, topics, services, and actions
- Simulate robots in Gazebo and Unity environments
- Integrate perception, planning, and control systems
- Apply AI algorithms for autonomous decision-making

**Course Structure:**
1. Introduction to Physical AI and Embodied Intelligence
2. Robotic Nervous System: ROS 2 fundamentals
3. Digital Twin: Gazebo & Unity simulations
4. AI Robot Brain: NVIDIA Isaac ROS
5. Vision-Language-Action (VLA)
6. Capstone Project: Autonomous Humanoid Robot
